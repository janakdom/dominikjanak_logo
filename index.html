Naming

The following table provides a simple nomenclature to identify the type of logo by it's short name.

Positions

The type of logo can be easily identified by its name. The first character is always l like 'logo'. The second can be b or s where b means 'big' logo and s means 'small' logo. The other characters are from left to right according to the table.
